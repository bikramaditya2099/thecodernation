HR CONCLAVE – Women & Future-of-Work

Rapid technological innovation, globalization, demographic shifts, climate change and geopolitical transformations are having an unprecedented impact on the work and skills landscape. On one hand, new technologies bring with them exciting opportunities, both for businesses in terms of their productivity, and for the workforce in terms of replacing unhealthy, dangerous and repetitive tasks with high-skilled work. On the other, as cognitive abilities and tasks that were once thought to be reserved for humans are increasingly being carried out by machines, there is a growing concern about the impact on jobs and the subsequent risks for government, business and people.

There is a window of opportunity now for individuals, business, academia and government to understand and proactively manage the transition to a new future. This conclave is to invite CHROs, Talent Heads, Business Leaders, Policy Makers and Academia to discuss about the challenges and what will help our workforce succeed in this ever changing world. International Women’s day being round the corner (March 8th), this Conclave is designed specifically to celebrate the contribution of Women in enhancing our workplaces and discuss Future-of-Work, particularly for the Women workforce.

Conclave will be followed by a 2 days Career Fair with focus on employment opportunities for Women!

HR CONCLAVE – Objective

This conclave aims to :

  • ✓ Exchange of views, thoughts, best practices and ideas on Future-of-Work, Industry wise & overall
  • ✓ Push towards an improvement in the entire Talent ecosystem in Odisha – Policy
  • ✓ Makers, Students, Working Professionals, Academia and Industry
  • ✓ Bridge the gap between the industry and academia by getting them on a single platform Showcase talent in Odisha
  • ✓ Bring jobs exclusively for the Women workforce of Odisha
